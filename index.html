BEST HOTEL CHAIN IN EAST AFRICA

The Citats Hotel is the winner of the 2015 best hotel chain in East Africa and the year's top international brand. This prestigious award comprises a number of categories in the tourism industry.

In conjunction with the Travel News magazine, Grand Travel Awards have been given since 1993. More than 100 travel agencies and income operators are given the chance to vote each year.
